What is a Computer?

A computer is an electronic machine that takes input, processes data, and produces output. It helps us perform tasks faster and more accurately.

Example: Typing a document, browsing the internet, or watching a video all require a computer.

Main Parts of a Computer

The main parts of a computer are:

  • Input Devices (Keyboard, Mouse)
  • CPU (Brain of the computer)
  • Memory (RAM, Hard Disk)
  • Output Devices (Monitor, Printer)

Why Learning Computer Basics is Important

Computer knowledge is required for almost every job today. Learning the basics helps in education, exams, and career growth.
