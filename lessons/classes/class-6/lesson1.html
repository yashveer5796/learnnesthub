Lesson 1: Introduction to Computers – basics, types, and examples

Introduction

In today’s world, computers are everywhere. From schools and offices to homes and hospitals, computers help us perform many tasks quickly and easily. Let us understand what a computer is and why it is so important.

What is a Computer?

A computer is an electronic machine that takes input, processes it, and gives output. It works according to the instructions given to it.

In simple words:

A computer helps us do work faster, more accurately, and with less effort.

Example:

  • When we type numbers and press calculate, the computer gives the answer.
  • When we type a document and save it, the computer stores it for future use.

Basic Working of a Computer

A computer works in three simple steps:

  1. Input – Data or instructions given to the computer
  2. Process – The computer works on the input
  3. Output – The result given by the computer

Example:

  • Input: Numbers typed using keyboard
  • Process: Calculation
  • Output: Result shown on screen

This is called the IPO Cycle (Input–Process–Output).

Characteristics of a Computer

1. Speed

A computer works very fast. It can perform millions of calculations in seconds.

2. Accuracy

A computer gives accurate results if the input and instructions are correct.

3. Storage

A computer can store a large amount of data such as documents, pictures, videos, and songs.

4. Diligence

A computer does not get tired. It can work continuously for a long time.

5. Versatility

A computer can perform different types of tasks like typing, drawing, calculating, and playing games.

Importance of Computers

Computers are important because they:

  • Save time and effort
  • Make work easy and fast
  • Help in learning and education
  • Are used in almost every field

Uses of Computers in Daily Life

  • Schools – Online classes, exams, projects
  • Hospitals – Patient records, reports
  • Banks – Managing accounts and transactions
  • Homes – Entertainment, online shopping, learning

Summary

  • A computer is an electronic machine
  • It works on input, process, and output
  • Computers are fast, accurate, and reliable
  • They are used in many fields of life

    • D. Storage

  3. Which characteristic means a computer works very fast?
    • A. Accuracy
    • B. Speed
    • C. Storage
    • D. Diligence
      • Answer: B
  4. Which place does NOT commonly use computers?
    • A. School
    • B. Hospital
