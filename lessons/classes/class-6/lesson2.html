Lesson 2: Types of Computers

Welcome to Lesson 2! In this lesson, we will explore the different types of computers and how they are used in various environments.

1. Personal Computer (PC)

Designed for individual use, a PC is suitable for tasks like word processing, browsing the internet, and gaming.

2. Laptop

A portable computer that allows you to work from anywhere. Laptops have batteries and are ideal for mobility.

3. Server

Servers manage network resources and store data for multiple users. They are commonly used in organizations and data centers.

4. Supercomputer

Supercomputers are extremely powerful machines used for complex scientific calculations, simulations, and research.

5. Mainframe Computer

Mainframes are used by large organizations to process massive amounts of data efficiently and reliably.
