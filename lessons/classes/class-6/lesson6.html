Lesson 6: Introduction to the Internet and Its Uses

Introduction

The Internet has become an important part of our daily life. It helps us connect with people, find information, and learn new things. In this lesson, we will understand what the Internet is and how it is used.

What is the Internet?

The Internet is a global network of computers that are connected to each other. It allows people all over the world to share information and communicate.

In simple words:

The Internet connects computers and helps us send and receive information.

Examples:

  • Sending emails
  • Searching information on Google
  • Watching online videos

Internet Services

The Internet provides many useful services:

  • Email: Used to send and receive messages
  • Websites: Used to read information and news
  • Online Learning: Attend classes and courses
  • Social Media: Connect with friends and family

Uses of the Internet

The Internet is used in many fields:

  • Education: Online classes, study materials
  • Communication: Email, video calls
  • Entertainment: Movies, music, games
  • Shopping: Buying products online

Advantages of the Internet

  • Easy access to information
  • Saves time and effort
  • Connects people worldwide
  • Helps in learning and research

Summary

  • The Internet is a global network of computers
  • It helps in communication and information sharing
  • Internet services include email and websites
  • It is used in education, business, and entertainment

MCQs (Practice Questions)

  1. What is the Internet?
    • A. A single computer
    • B. A global network of computers
    • C. A software
    • D. An input device
      • Answer: B
  2. Which service is used to send messages online?
    • A. Email
    • B. Calculator
    • C. Paint
    • D. Printer
      • Answer: A
  3. Which of the following is a use of the Internet?
    • A. Cooking food
    • B. Online learning
    • C. Washing clothes
    • D. Driving a car
      • Answer: B
  4. Which is NOT an advantage of the Internet?
    • A. Easy access to information
    • B. Saves time
    • C. Makes people lazy always
    • D. Connects people worldwide
      • Answer: C
