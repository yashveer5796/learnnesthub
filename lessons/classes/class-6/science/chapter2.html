Introduction

The food we eat every day contains different substances that are needed by our body. These substances are called nutrients.

What Are Nutrients?

Nutrients are the useful components of food that help our body to grow, get energy, and stay healthy.

Main Components of Food

The major nutrients present in food are:

  • Carbohydrates
  • Proteins
  • Fats
  • Vitamins
  • Minerals
  • Roughage
  • Water
