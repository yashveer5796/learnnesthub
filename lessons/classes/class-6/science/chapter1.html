Chapter 1: Food – Where Does It Come From?

Learn this chapter lesson-wise using accordion. Your progress is saved automatically.

Food is anything we eat or drink to stay alive and healthy.

  • Gives energy
  • Helps in growth
  • Protects from diseases

Food comes from two main sources:

  1. Plants
  2. Animals

We eat different parts of plants:

  • Roots – Carrot
  • Leaves – Spinach
  • Fruits – Mango
  • Seeds – Wheat

Animals give us:

  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Meat
  • Honey

Herbivores: Eat plants
Carnivores: Eat animals
Omnivores: Eat both
