Introduction

Food is essential for all living beings. Every day we eat different kinds of food like rice, chapati, vegetables, fruits, milk, and eggs. But have you ever wondered where this food comes from?

  • What food is
  • Sources of food

What is Food?

Food is anything that we eat or drink to stay alive and healthy.

  • Gives us energy
  • Helps us grow
  • Keeps us healthy and strong

Why Do We Need Food?

  • Gives energy
  • Helps in growth and repair
  • Keeps us active

Without food, living beings cannot survive.
